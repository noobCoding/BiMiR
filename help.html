Search Options

miRNA

  • A list of all available micro-RNAs in BiMiR database.
  • Specific one micro-RNA to limit the searching range.
  • Leave it blank to display all available biclusters in database.

Organ/Tissue

  • A list of all organs/tissues in BiMiR database.
  • Choose one organ/tissue to specific your interest.
  • Capability to combine with miRNA session for searching.

Disease

  • A list of all associated diseases in BiMiR database.
  • Choose one disease specific your interest.
  • Capability to combine with miRNA session for searching.

Keywords & Specific Genes

  • An advanced search option.
  • Input any keyword of interest to retrieve related conditions and biclusters.
  • A prefix ‘+’ will emphasize your interest to ‘must have’ degree.
  • A prefix ‘-‘ indicates ‘not interested’ term.
  • Put interested phrase between “…” to search for exact desire phrase.
  • Capability to combine with miRNA session for searching.


Search Results

Name

  • Name and link to details of a bicluster.

miRNA

  • Name of microRNA including this bicluster.

Fold

  • Cut-off threshold of a bicluster.

Size

  • Size of bicluster presented as (number_of_conditions x number_of_targets).

Validated

  • The fraction of validated evidence (miRTarBase).

Proportion

  • Available in searching with ‘Organ/Tissue’ and ‘Disease’ specification.
  • Indicate the frequency of interested organ/tissue/disease appearing in this bicluster.

Targets

  • List of associated targets

Enriched Pathways

  • List of significant enriched pathways (adjusted p-value < 0.1) from C2 and C5 sets.


Bicluster Details

Bicluster brief information

  • Bicluster name is in BLUE.
  • Name of miRNA is in GREEN.
  • Fold number is in RED.

Bicluster map

  • A heat map indicates activation which is scaled between RED and GREEN.
  • Each row has a link ta specific condition series from NCBI GEO.
  • Each column has a link ttarget/gene description from Gdatabase.
  • Degree of each node (target) in interactive network is shown.
  • Evidence of tests are alsindicated.
  • A link to network visualization of this bicluster is highlighted in VIOLET.

Keyword cloud

  • High frequency terms are extracted from all conditions tbriefly describe the bicluster.
  • Each term owns a link tGoogle definition.

Experimental Conditions

  • Brief descriptions of experiment conditions are alsprovided for quick reference.

Functional Enrichment Test

  • List of all significant enriched pathways from C2 and C5 sets.


Network Visualization

Bicluster Information

  • Bicluster name is in BLUE.
  • Name of miRNA is in GREEN.
  • Fold number is in RED.

Network

  • A color scale indicates the MAXIMUM and MINIMUM connections in this network.
  • Color of a gene node is scaled based on its degree (connection count).
  • Thickness of an edge indicates the strength of the connection.
